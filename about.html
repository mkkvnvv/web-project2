About me

Good morning to those who read this in the morning, and good evening to those who visited this site in the evening! My name is Sasha, I am 19 years old and I lived my 16 years in Ukraine after which I began to build a life in a small town in Scotland. Now I am studying at City of Glasgow college for Digital Design, and I am very happy that I am receiving this particular education! My creativity is manifested in many areas, for example, photography - I really love to photograph the streets of different countries and cities, people, animals and various little things. I can convey the beauty of any object through photography, be it old architecture or an ordinary trash can. I also love to write music, shoot videos, draw, play instruments, knit and even try to write my own book,

in short - to do something with my own hands.

I know several languages, so I think I can come to an agreement with absolutely any person! I speak Ukrainian, English, Polish and Russian. Just like all people, I have a dream! I dream of becoming a sought-after designer in all its fields, I like design, I like to create something with my own hands and share it with people, I want to become an inspiration for someone and make a big contribution to our common future.
